## Color palette

The IBM Services brand focuses primarily on the blue and gray values within the IBM color palette. While all blues and grays are available for use, the core colors for IBM Services are Black, Blue 80, Blue 60 and White.

Additional colors from the IBM palette may be used as accents when needed. The complete IBM color palette is shown on the following page.
